Frequently Asked Questions

Is Piik.me completely free to use?

Yes, Piik.me is open-source and free for the community. You get access to core features without hidden paywalls.

Can I use my own custom domain?

Absolutely. You can easily map your own branded domains to ensure your links look professional and trustworthy.

How accurate are the analytics?

Our analytics run in real-time. We provide highly accurate, granular data on geography, devices, and referrers.
